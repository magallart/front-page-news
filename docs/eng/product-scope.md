# Product Scope

This document summarizes the current product boundaries of Front Page News.

## Core product

Front Page News is a metadata aggregation and discovery product.

It is built to help users:

- scan current headlines quickly
- navigate by section
- navigate by publisher
- search across aggregated news
- preview a story before leaving the app

## Included capabilities

- editorial home page
- section pages
- source pages
- search results pages
- quick-view modal
- local filter persistence
- background refresh feedback

## Explicit exclusions

- full article scraping
- full article mirroring
- user accounts
- comments or social features
- paywall bypass

## Editorial principle

The app should always preserve:

- source attribution
- clear outbound reading
- graceful handling of missing or imperfect feed data
